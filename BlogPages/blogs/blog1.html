The Library of Babel.

March 15 2021 by Maria Auditore. Before I get started on The Library of Babel I would like to introduce myself. My name Maria Auditore an Italian girl with grey eyes and black hair that moved from Venice Italy when I was 4 years old to Johannesburg South Africa. I wear glasses because I cannot see, my hand writing is almost unreadable and I am a secret assassin, but you would never know by looking at my shy demeanour. Being an assassin is not the only thing I do, as I work part time in a library and due to this I thought it would be appropriate to talk about the Library of Babel and how it compares to that of the internet as we know it.
